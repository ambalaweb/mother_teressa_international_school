GENERAL DISCIPLINE

The school believes in discipline and good manners, tries and inculcates a sense of responsibility. Discipline distinguishes an upright person. We tried to instill discipline in the routine life of our students. In this regard certain rules are enforced.

•   Students are not allowed to bring valuables in school.

•   Expensive Jewelry is not allowed to be worn by any student.

•   All children must wear proper uniform, which includes proper shoes also.

•   Fees must be paid in time.

•   Students are not allowed to leave the school during school timings, except during an emergency.

   Co-operation of parents in these matters is of paramount importance, which is, therefore, expected and requested.
